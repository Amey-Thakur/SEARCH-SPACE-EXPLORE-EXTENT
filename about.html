Search Space | Explore Extent Leadership

India decided to go to space when Indian National Committee for Space Research (INCOSPAR) was set up by the Government of India in 1962. With the visionary Dr Vikram Sarabhai at its helm, INCOSPAR set up the Thumba Equatorial Rocket Launching Station (TERLS) in Thiruvananthapuram for upper atmospheric research.

Search Space is led by Administrator Amey Thakur, Search Space's 1st administrator. Before building Space Search Organisation, Amey served in the NBA, representing Laker’s First MVP, serving on the NBA Services Committee and the Science, Space and Technology Committee. Amey’s career in Space Research began in the NBA.
